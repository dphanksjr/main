I'm an independent video game designer from San Francisco currently residing in Toronto.

I have over 13 years of Level Design, Systems Design, Story & Character Development, Scripting and Lead Level Design experience.

I’ve worked on such games as: Holeshot Drag Racing, Zombie Apocalypse, Conan, America’s Army, RTX: Red Rock, Star Wars: Episode 3, Indiana Jones and the Infernal Machine and others for iPhone, PS3, XBox 360, XBLA, PSN, PS2 and PC.

My goal is to create appealing and fun interactive experiences while keeping in mind the scope of the project and the studio's tools & technology.
